My Learning Agreement Defined

December 02, 2018 by Natalie

This agreement is designed to help achieve my learning based targets & further develop my skills

Understanding my strengths and limitations is important in formulating a clear learning plan, it will enable me to recognise what I am good at and develop these traits to achieve better results. My strengths can be defined as:

  • Creative thinker
  • Empathetic
  • Analytical

My limitations can be defined as:

  • Dislike conflict
  • Tendency to overthink things/get 'too in my head'

Considering this I am committed to constantly being open to critical feedback from other students and facilitators, and to in turn provide feedback where necessary. Although I see my creative side as a strength, I will make sure that the foundations/concepts are more important than getting too caught up in the design in order not to fall in to the rabbit hole (so to speak). I acknowledge that I need to have a clear understanding of principles and be able to describe concepts in analogies/plain English, I will make sure to add this to my reflections to further solidify my learning.

Where I get stuck on certain problems, if I cannot find a solution after first trying myself I will utilise the Slack Channel to seek feedback, and ensure where I do come across any problems I resolve them in a timely manner so that this does not affect my learning plan.

From the facilitation team I expect support when I am struggling, and constructive feedback on my work.
